Rice recipe

Description

Whenever Christmas approaches I am reminded of the various dishes that my relatives usually bring to family parties, but one that is never missing and everyone loves is the recipe for simple raisin rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 4 cups boiling water
  • 1/2 unit of yellow onion
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup of raisins

Steps

  1. In a pan over medium heat, heat the olive oil and add the onion;
  2. Let it stew until it starts to become transparent;
  3. Add the garlic and saute a little more until golden brown;
  4. Add the rice and let it cook for 1 minute;
  5. Add the hot water and mix well;
  6. Season with salt and mix well;
  7. Add the raisins and mix well;
  8. Cover the pan partially and let it cook on low heat;
  9. When the water dries out completely, turn off the heat and the rice is ready to serve.
