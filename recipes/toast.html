Toast recipe

Description

There always comes a day of the week when that bread roll is left over and we don't know what to do with it, right? So today I'm going to share step by step how to make a simple homemade toast, very easy to do, super fast and tasty.

Ingredients

  • 3 pieces of crusty bread
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic

Steps

  1. In a bowl place the butter (at room temperature), the garlic, a pinch of salt, the oregano, and knead well;
  2. Cut the bread into slices approximately 1 cm thick;
  3. Spread the seasoned mixture on one side of the rolls;
  4. Place the bread in a baking pan (with the butter on top);
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown;
  6. Now just let it cool and serve.
