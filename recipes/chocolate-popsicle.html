Chocolate Popsicle recipe

Description

Do you want a simple, quick, and practical way to cool off in the heat? You just found it!

Ingredients

  • 1 of chocolate powder
  • 1L of milk
  • 6 spoons of powdered milk
  • 1 box of cream
  • 1 box of condensed milk
  • Granulated chocolate

Steps

  1. In a blender add the milk, chocolate milk, condensed milk, heavy cream, and powdered milk;
  2. Blend for 2-3 minutes or until smooth;
  3. Then place a spoonful of granulated popsicles in each cup (this recipe yields 12 popsicles);
  4. Pour the contents of the blender, distributing them in all the cups and place in the freezer or refrigerator for 1h30 or until ice crystals begin to form;
  5. Skewer the toothpicks and leave for another 3 hours;
  6. Finally, remove the cups by cutting them with scissors;
  7. There you go, now just enjoy and refresh yourself.
