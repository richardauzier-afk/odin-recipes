Spaghetti recipe

Description

This is another recipe for beginners and also for those who can't take any more mistakes when making noodles at home. You will learn here, once and for all, how to make simple noodles with quick tips.

Ingredients

  • 250g of noodle
  • 2 and 1/2 L of water
  • 1 spoon (soup) of sault

Steps

  1. Place the water in a saucepan over medium heat;
  2. When it starts boiling add the salt;
  3. Then pour the noodles into the pot all at once;
  4. Then let it cook until the pasta is al dente;
  5. Then turn off the heat and strain the noodles through a sieve;
  6. After that serve the noodles still hot with sauce or as desired.
